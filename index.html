About Me

Hi! I'm Sheilla Olga, a Technical Product Marketing Manager who believes the best stories come from understanding bothtechnology and the people it serves. I chose Nairobi for this project because it represents what excites me most about tech: innovation that solves real problems for real people.

About website

From M-Pesa handling over $310 billion in transactions globally to Nairobi startups securing $638 million in funding in 2024 , this city proves that great product marketing isn't about following Silicon Valley—it's about understanding your market deeply enough to create category-defining solutions. This website showcases Nairobi's transformation from "safari destination" to Silicon Savannah with over 152 startups spawned from innovation hubs . It's designed to challenge preconceptions and highlight how innovation happens when technology meets genuine human need—a principle that drives my approach to technical product marketing.
